How it works

The Loadout View allows you to search for multiple items at once to build a complete gear set.

Select items for each slot (Head, Chest, Feet, Weapon, Offhand), choose your desired tier, and add them to your loadout.

The tool will find the cheapest equivalent tier for each item and show you the total cost of your loadout.
